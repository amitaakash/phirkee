Ambiente Frankfurt is one of the largest and most prestigious handicrafts exhibition and India was chosen as the Partner Country. The Office of the Development Commissioner (Handicrafts) was tasked with coordinating and popularising India’s participation.

The challenge was that their social media pages were defunct and barely had any followers. To add to it we were told that there was no media budget available for the campaign. The task was to increase followers organically – a tough ask given the low reach offered by social media channels which want to maximise revenues.
